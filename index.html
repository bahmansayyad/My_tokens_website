About

IRANOIL is a community-driven token on the TON blockchain, inspired by the value of oil as a global resource. The project aims to build strong liquidity, enable trading across DEX platforms, and provide long-term utility through partnerships and integrations.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 20,000,000,000 IRANOIL
  • 50% distributed across 21 wallets (community holders)
  • 20% allocated for DEX liquidity (phased release)
  • 10% reserved for team and advisors
  • 10% marketing & partnerships
  • 10% strategic reserve with burn mechanism
